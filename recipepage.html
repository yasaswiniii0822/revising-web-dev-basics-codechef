Chocolate Cake

A simple and delicious chocolate cake recipe for beginners.
Perfect for birthdays and celebrations!

Prep Time: 20 minutes
Cook Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup of milk

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°c.
  2. Slowly mix the flour, sugar, and cocoa powder.
  3. Add eggs and milk, stir until smooth.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.

Tips

For extra flavor, add chocolate chips before baking. You can also serve with vanilla ice cream for a delicious combo!
